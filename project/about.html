About This Project

Project Subject: Museums in Montevideo. This site is a digital guide intended to help residents and visitors discover the city's museums, their hours, and cultural offerings.

Planned Features

  • Homepage with introduction and featured museums.
  • Gallery and directory with photos and descriptions.
  • Interactive map and zone filtering (Leaflet + OpenStreetMap).
  • Real-time "Open Now" status based on local time.

Reason for Selecting the Subject

Montevideo hosts a diverse museum scene that is often under-discovered. The goal is to provide an accessible, mobile-friendly guide promoting cultural engagement.
